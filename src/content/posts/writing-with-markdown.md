Lipi supports standard Markdown and MDX.

## Headings

## Heading 2
### Heading 3

## Paragraphs

Typography in Lipi is optimized for comfortable longform reading with generous line-height and restrained spacing.

- Timeline feeds

Lipi intentionally keeps markdown rendering clean and lightweight without excessive documentation-style components.
