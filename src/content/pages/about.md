Lipi is a minimal editorial theme for Astro built around typography, chronology, and calm reading experiences.

It is designed for:

- essays
- developer journals
- travel writing
- digital publishing
- personal archives

The theme intentionally avoids dashboard-style layouts and excessive UI complexity.

Instead, Lipi focuses on:

- reading rhythm
- whitespace
- lightweight performance
- markdown-native workflows

Built with Astro and Tailwind CSS.
